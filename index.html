About Dalgona Coffee

Dalgona coffee is a South Korean coffee dish, which went viral during the Lockdown. The dish can be made from a few recipes which are ready available in your kitchen cupboard. What are you waiting for?

Ingedients

  • Instant Coffee powder or Expresso Granules
  • Hot Water
  • Sugar
  • Milk

Steps

Step 1: Add coffee powder and hot water
Add 2 tabelspoons of coffee powder and a cup of hot water. Add 2 tabelspoons of sugar to the mixture
Step 2: To stir the mixture well
Use a handheld mixer and stir the mixture well,till it turns creamy and fluffy
Step 3: Serve it with Milk
Add a cup of hot milk and add the fluffy mixture to the milk. Serve it with a spoon and a straw. Now stir to enjoy the dish!
